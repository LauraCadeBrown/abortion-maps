Out of State Abortion Rates, 2012-2015

This map was generated in QGIS by mapping CDC abortion rates onto state geography and georeferencing abortion clinics. The map illustrates the rate of out of state abortions and the most readily known abortion sites, as listed online by two sources.

The data was taken from CDC reports and from the Abortion Federation and a pro-life site listing Planned Parenthood clinics.

  • See the map: Out of State Abortion Rates
  • Data Source: CDC, Abortion Surveillance System
